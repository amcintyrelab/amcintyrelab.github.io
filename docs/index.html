Research ▼

mRNA regulation in B cells

We are working on understanding how nuclear condensates and cytoplasmic interactions affect the production of inflammatory molecules

Cellular heterogeneity in adaptive immune disease

Using proximity labelling and high-throughput imaging approaches, we are characterizing cellular heterogeneity in patient and donor samples

- Bhamidipati, McIntyre, et al., 2025

RNA therapeutics for autoimmune disease

We are developing pre-clinical targeted approaches to reduce autoreactivity using short oligonucleotides and optimizing delivery to primary B cells

Other interests

We are interested in the uncharacterized immune systems of odd and less studied single-cell creatures
